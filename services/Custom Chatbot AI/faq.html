Personalized interactions refer to the ability of AI chatbots to tailor their responses and assistance based on a user's unique needs and preferences. This helps create a customized experience that builds customer loyalty and satisfaction.

AI chatbots can handle a high volume of customer queries at once, reducing the workload on human agents. This helps improve response times and lower costs, resulting in a more efficient and effective customer support team.
